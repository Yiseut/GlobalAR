# Music Selection Criteria

The previous local generated track was selected with the wrong priority:
low interference and no third-party samples were weighted too heavily, while
energy and brand fit were underweighted.

Better criteria for this dashboard demo:

- Rights: royalty-free, no local copyrighted music by default.
- Energy: medium tempo, approximately 95-120 BPM, not drone-like.
- Tone: premium business / consulting / technology, clean and confident.
- Texture: light pulse, restrained percussion, warm synth or piano accents.
- Mix: no vocal lead, no heavy bass, leaves room for UI details.
- Structure: audible lift every 8-12 seconds so scene changes feel intentional.
- Avoid: white noise beds, slow ambient pads, sleep/meditation textures.

The next music replacement should be chosen against these criteria.
